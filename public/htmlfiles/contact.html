What to include

Useful details when you write to us

  • The branch and semester you are studying.
  • The subject or resource type you want help with.
  • A clear note if you are reporting an issue or suggesting a correction.
  • Any useful links, file names, or screenshots that make the request easier to review.
We try to keep the project student-first, so short and specific messages are easier to act on than long general requests.
