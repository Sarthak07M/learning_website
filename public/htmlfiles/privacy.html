Privacy Policy

How IET Resources handles privacy, cookies, and AdSense.

This page explains the basic privacy practices for a public educational website that uses Google AdSense and standard web analytics behavior.

Cookies and ads

Google AdSense may use cookies

IET Resources displays Google AdSense ads on supported pages. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads, including personalized advertising where applicable.

  • Cookies help the site remember basic settings and improve performance.
  • Ad delivery may use information about your visit to show relevant ads.
  • You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

Information use

Educational content first

Public resources

The study material on this site is intended for educational use by students.

Contact messages

Email messages are used only to respond to student support or contribution requests.

Site stability

Basic logs and analytics help keep the website working and improve page quality.

If you have privacy questions or want a resource reviewed, contact the team with the page link and a short explanation.
