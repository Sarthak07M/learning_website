Privacy Policy

A simple privacy policy for a student-focused educational site.

This page explains the general principles used by IET Resources when users visit pages, read content, or interact with resource links.

What we collect

Basic site usage only

IET Resources is primarily a public educational website. We may receive ordinary server logs, browser details, and analytics data that help us keep the site working properly and understand which study pages are useful.

  • We do not ask for unnecessary personal data to read or use the study materials.
  • Contact details are only used when you choose to email the team.
  • Uploaded resources are handled for educational review and organization.

How information is used

To improve learning and site quality

Site improvement

Feedback and usage patterns help us improve navigation, fix broken links, and organize content more clearly.

Educational support

Resource contributions are reviewed so notes, papers, and study files remain useful for students.

AdSense readiness

We keep the site content-focused, easy to navigate, and transparent about how the website works.

If you have a privacy concern or want content removed, contact the team and include the relevant page link so it can be checked quickly.
